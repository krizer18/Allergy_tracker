Allergy Tracker Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2025

Introduction

Allergy Tracker is a Chrome extension designed to help users identify potential allergens in their Amazon Fresh cart items. This privacy policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and what choices you have regarding your data.

Information We Collect

Allergy Tracker collects the following information:

  • User Account Information: Email address for authentication purposes.
  • Allergy Information: The allergies you add to your profile.
  • Scan Results: Information about products in your Amazon Fresh cart, including product titles, URLs, and ingredients.

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To authenticate your identity and provide access to your saved allergies.
  • To analyze product ingredients and identify potential allergens.
  • To save your allergies and scan results for future reference.

Data Storage

Your data is stored in two locations:

  • Local Storage: We store your allergies and user information in your browser's local storage for persistence between sessions.
  • Firebase Firestore: If you create an account, your allergies and scan results are also stored in our Firebase Firestore database to enable access across devices.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your information to outside parties. Your data is only used within the extension to provide the allergy tracking service.

Amazon Data Access

This extension accesses and processes information from Amazon.com product pages to extract ingredient information. We only access pages that you are actively viewing in your browser, and only when you explicitly request a scan of your cart.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Opt out of using the extension at any time

Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the extension.
